FinTech Plus reported strong Q2 2023 results with $125 million in revenue, a 25% year-over-year increase. Gross profit margin was 58%, and EBITDA reached $37.5 million with a 30% margin. Net income rose to $16 million, up from $10 million in Q2 2022. The company expanded its product line, including high-yield savings and RoboAdvisor platforms, and invested in various securities. Total assets were $1.5 billion with a debt-to-equity ratio of 1.5. Customer acquisition costs dropped by 15%, and LTV-CAC ratio improved to 3.5x. Risk management measures include a value at risk model and a conservative leverage approach. The company forecasts $135 million in Q3 revenue, driven by blockchain and AI solutions, and announced the upcoming IPO of its PayPlus subsidiary, expected to raise $200 million.
